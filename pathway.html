Pathway Intermediates is a Korea-based animal nutrition company developing functional feed additives for poultry, swine, and ruminants — covering absorption, gut health, immunity, and stress management.

In Bangladesh, our regional team works with feed mills, integrators, and farms across broiler, layer, and dairy operations — bringing proven science to the country's hot, humid climate.
